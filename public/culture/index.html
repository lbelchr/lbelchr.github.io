Culture

Culture is defined by more than just the benefits and key value buzzwords that are put on a job posting. Culture is the recognition that people are the most important part of any community, and it represents an investment in those people.

I believe in a culture that fosters the balance between challenge and skill for each individual. It should treat people as adults, and encourage independence and accountability. It should value transparency and communication as the most important tools for facilitating responsible growth.
